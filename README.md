
# 🍽️ Restaurant Management System (RMS)

## 📌 Project Overview

This project aims to build a complete Restaurant Management System that supports restaurant daily operations and helps transform operational data into meaningful business insights. The system combines transactional processing with analytical reporting to improve efficiency and decision making.

---

## 🎯 Objectives

* Improve restaurant operational workflow
* Manage orders, menu items, and reservations
* Monitor inventory and supplier activities
* Track employee and sales performance
* Support business decisions using analytics and reporting

---

## ⚙️ System Modules

### 🍔 Menu Management

Manage menu categories, pricing, and available items.

### 🧾 Order Management

Handle customer orders and transaction records.

### 📅 Reservation & Seating

Organize reservations and optimize seating arrangements.

### 📦 Inventory Management

Track stock levels and monitor inventory movement.

### 👨‍💼 Employee Management

Store employee information and support operational tracking.

### 📊 Reporting & Analytics

Generate reports and business insights from collected data.

---

## 🏗️ Data Architecture

The project follows a layered approach:

* **Operational Database (OLTP)** → Daily restaurant transactions
* **Data Warehouse** → Store processed business data
* **Business Intelligence Layer** → Reporting and analytical queries

---

## 📈 Business Intelligence

The system provides analytical capabilities to answer business questions such as:

* Which menu items generate the highest sales?
* What are customer ordering patterns?
* How does inventory affect operations?
* What trends can support business growth?
